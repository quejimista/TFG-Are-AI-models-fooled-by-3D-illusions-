Tested on extraflat world for easier building. I decided the floor to be Gray concrete, the reason is to avoid possible biases with blocks like Grass or dirt,
which have patterns. This is a solid block.


- FOV: 70 (Default)
- Lighting: Smooth Lighting (MAX): Create better shadows.
- Clouds: OFF
- Particles: MINIMUM
- Fog: OFF
- Entity shadows: OFF
- Graphics: Fancy
- Dynamic Lighting: NO

I strictly do each time /time set noon to place the sun just above 90º
F1 to hide the HUD

I originally thought in downloading shaders (They upgrade the graphical aspect of the game and add better shadows). However, I discarded the idea because they add unwanted
effects such as bloom, lens flare, etc. Potentially making the results worse. I have seen that they may also unfocus the further blocks.
